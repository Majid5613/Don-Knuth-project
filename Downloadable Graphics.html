Downloadable Graphics

This page contains some encapsulated PostScript ® files that you might enjoy. They can either be printed as pages by themselves or included in TeX documents (using the epsf macros of dvips). If your browser automatically shows you the image instead of letting you download the source files, try ``shift click'' instead of ``click.'' By editing the first few lines of the PostScript code you can easily change the colors and/or shades of gray. Artwork by Duane Bibby
